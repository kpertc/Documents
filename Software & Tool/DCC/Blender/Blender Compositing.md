### Backdrop Image

`Ctrl` / `Command` + `Shift` View in the background / Backdrop
`V` zooms out
`Alt` / `Option` + `V` zooms in
`Alt` / `Option` + `MMB` pan
`Alt` / `Option` + `Home` fits the frame

![[img/Blender Compositing/backdrop-image.png]]
